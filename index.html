Her Legacy

A Legacy That
Continues to Inspire

Brentrice Wilson was a remarkable 16-year-old young lady whose life ended abruptly. Despite her young age, she had already made a significant impact on those around her. She was known for her intelligence, loving nature, generosity, sense of humor, and authenticity.

Wise beyond her years, Brentrice demonstrated extraordinary responsibility by working two jobs while still in high school. She faithfully saved money, generously helped others, and dreamed of becoming a lawyer before later developing a passion for entrepreneurship.

To honor her life, Wilson Family Ministries established the Brentrice Wilson Scholarship. This scholarship continues her passion for helping others by investing in students who desire to create meaningful change in their communities.

"LOVE WINS. Even through tragedy, we can build hope by investing in the next generation."
